RM /unit basis, Energy /unit basis, Machine OH /unit basis and Film total cost /unit basis are expressed in the same pricing unit as the line basis (kg gross, kg net, or roll).
Unit weight net (kg) = net film weight per unit (excluding core weight and all packing materials).
Film total cost /unit basis = film total cost (per kg net) × Unit weight net, converted to the same pricing unit basis.
Pack+core /unit = packing materials cost per unit (from packing_items) + core cost (core price per kg × core weight from the quotation), converted to the same basis.
Total cost /unit = Film total cost /unit basis + Pack+core /unit.
Margin% = base margin from pricing rules.
Net Margin% (after discount) = Margin% − line discount%.
Extra /unit comes from machine extras (color on gross, prestretch on net) converted to the same basis.
EXW /unit = Total cost /unit + Margin value /unit + Extra /unit (after applying any payment term credit to the invoice price).
FOB cost /unit and Sea Freight cost /unit are converted from cost per kg to the same pricing unit (kg gross, kg net, or roll), according to the line basis.
FOB /unit = EXW /unit + FOB cost /unit, and CFR /unit = FOB /unit + Sea Freight cost /unit.
All “/unit” values in this table follow the selected pricing basis for each line (kg gross, kg net, or roll).
The green FOB/CFR /unit values are the final selling prices after applying any rounding on the gross kg unit price (where applicable).
The small gray values under FOB /unit and CFR /unit are the raw prices before rounding, expressed in the same pricing unit basis as the line (kg gross, kg net, or roll).
